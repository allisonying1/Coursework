My name is Allison Ying. I am a Seattle based Web Developer currently pursuing a masters degree in Computer Science at Northeastern University. My background is in Geography, Environmental Science, and Map-making. My pivot into Computer Scince is rooted in my passion for Digital Media and Design.

I believe that online communities are a source of solidarity, inspiration, and creative learning for individuals all over the world. I am further motivated by the accessibility of digital resources, which can be utilized to bridge and overcome social, economic, and enivonrmental barries in our real world. Moving forward, I would like to build websites that encourage self-learning, creative expression, and diverse collaboration.
